Orange Cats Studio — Security Policy

Security Policy

Last updated: May 2025

1. Scope

This policy applies to all digital properties operated by Orange Cats Studio, including our website at orangecatsstudio.com and any associated subdomains or web applications.

2. Reporting a Vulnerability

If you believe you have found a security vulnerability in any of our systems, please let us know as soon as possible. We take all reports seriously and will work to address confirmed issues promptly.

Contact:
Email: info&orangecatsstudio.com
Subject line: Security Vulnerability Report

Please include the following in your report:

  • A clear description of the vulnerability
  • Steps to reproduce the issue
  • The potential impact of the vulnerability
  • Any proof-of-concept code or screenshots (if applicable)

3. Responsible Disclosure

We ask that you:

  • Give us reasonable time to investigate and fix the issue before disclosing it publicly
  • Avoid accessing, modifying or deleting data that does not belong to you
  • Do not perform denial-of-service attacks or spam
  • Do not use the vulnerability for personal gain

We will acknowledge receipt of your report within 2 business days and aim to provide a status update within 7 business days.

4. What We Commit To

  • We will respond to your report promptly and keep you informed of our progress
  • We will not take legal action against researchers who follow this policy
  • We will credit researchers who responsibly disclose issues (if they wish to be credited)

5. Out of Scope

The following are outside the scope of this policy:

  • Social engineering attacks targeting our staff or clients
  • Physical security issues
  • Issues in third-party services we rely on (please report those directly to the provider)
  • Theoretical vulnerabilities with no practical impact

6. Languages

Reports are accepted in English, Bosnian, Croatian, Serbian or German.
