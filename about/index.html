Our story

Why Orange Cats?

We spent years inside other people's companies watching good ideas die in committee, watching mediocre work ship with a straight face, and watching founders get charged agency rates for junior output. We got tired of it.

Orange Cats Studio started as an experiment: what if two experienced people just... did the work directly? No overhead, no layers, no fluff. Just real output, clear communication, and honest pricing.

Turns out people liked it. So we kept going. And as the services business grew, we started building our own things — the game, the boat platform — because that's what you do when you love building. You build.

The cats part? Our cats basically run the studio. The orange part? Well, have you seen our brand?

What we believe

The things we won't compromise on.

Honesty > comfort

We'd rather tell you something you don't want to hear than watch you make a bad decision with wrong information. We're not always right. But we're always honest.

Ship, then improve

Perfect is the enemy of shipped. We bias toward getting something real into the world and iterating — rather than planning endlessly in a doc nobody reads.

Quality has a standard

We move fast but we don't cut corners. The work we ship reflects who we are. Mediocre output isn't an option, even under deadline pressure.

Build in public

We document our journey because we believe transparency makes the whole ecosystem better. Our wins and our failures are on the record — follow along.

Community over audience

We're not chasing follower counts. We want the right people — builders, founders, curious humans — who actually engage with what we make.

Stay weird

The most interesting companies in the world have personality. We'd rather be genuinely ourselves than polished and forgettable.

About Orange Cats Studio — Independent Digital Studio

Orange Cats Studio is an independent entertainment and digital product studio founded by two experienced practitioners who wanted to do excellent work without corporate constraints. We offer web development, SEO strategy, content creation, and digital growth consulting — and we're building original products including an indie game and a platform for the boating community.

We operate on a simple philosophy: do real work, communicate clearly, and care about the outcome. Our studio documents its journey publicly across TikTok, YouTube, and our podcast, because we believe building in public is better for the whole creative and technology community.
