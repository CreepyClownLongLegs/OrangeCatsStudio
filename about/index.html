What is
Orange Cats Studio?

Orange Cats Studio is a digital marketing studio for businesses that need a clearer and stronger online presence. We build Websites, improve SEO Visibility and create Digital Marketing strategies that support long-term online growth.

Website·SEO Visibility·Digital Strategy

Who are the founders of Orange Cats Studio?

Founder One

Web Development & Data Lead

Builds custom websites for businesses that need more than a good-looking page. The focus is clean code, stable structure and reliable tracking, so the website is easier to manage, measure and improve after launch.

Founder Two

Digital Marketing & Strategy Lead

Uses search demand, market research and performance data to set clear marketing priorities across SEO Visibility and paid marketing. The focus is knowing what to improve, where to appear and where budget is worth investing.

Why did we start Orange Cats Studio?

Orange Cats Studio started from a problem we saw too often in digital projects: websites were built first, while SEO, data, content and digital marketing strategy were considered later.

For a business, that can become expensive. A website may look finished, but once marketing teams start working with it, important gaps often appear: unclear page structure, missing tracking, weak content hierarchy, technical limitations or no clear visibility strategy.

When those foundations are missing, businesses lose time and budget fixing things that should have been planned before launch.

Orange Cats Studio was created to close that gap. We bring web development, data and digital marketing strategy into the same process, so websites are built with the next steps already in mind.

What does Orange Cats Studio stand for?

Built around the business

The website needs to fit the business behind it, not the other way around. Orange Cats Studio looks at the offer, the audience, the goals and the online presence before deciding what to build, redesign or improve.

No template thinking

Orange Cats Studio does not believe every business needs the same page layout with different colours. Structure, content and user experience should be shaped around what the business actually needs.

Marketing thinking from the start

A website should not be built first and questioned later. SEO visibility, content structure, data tracking and digital marketing strategy should be part of the planning, not just post-launch phases.

Clear decisions over vague advice

Businesses need to know what is a priority. Orange Cats Studio gives clear recommendations, explains why they matter and helps separate what needs attention from ideas that can wait.

Careful use of time and budget

Fixing avoidable mistakes costs money. Orange Cats Studio focuses on planning the right structure from the start so businesses can reduce unnecessary corrections and use budget more deliberately.

Built, measured and improved

Launch is not where the work should stop. Orange Cats Studio builds websites with the next steps in mind, so SEO visibility and marketing activity can be tracked and improved over time.
