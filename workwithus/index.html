How working with Orange Cats Studio starts? 🐾

Before client work begins, the scope, timeline, responsibilities and payment structure are clarified. This keeps the process focused and helps both sides understand what happens next.

1

Intro call

The first step is a short intro call to understand the business, the current situation and which type of support may be needed.

2

Proposal

After the call, Orange Cats Studio sends a proposal with the recommended scope, pricing, timeline, included services and required next steps.

3

First invoice

Once the proposal is accepted and the first invoice is paid, the client work officially begins.

4

Work phase

The agreed work starts, whether the scope covers website design and development, SEO Visibility, Digital Marketing Strategy or selected add-ons.

5

Review

The work is reviewed with the client, feedback is collected and the next adjustments, final steps or follow-up recommendations are defined.
